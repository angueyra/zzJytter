Pre-Kuhn (before 1963)

Logical positivism: loosely-knit group of philosophers and scientists -> Vienna Circle (led by Moritz Schlick, in 20s and 30s). Fled Nazi Germany and WWII, many emigrated to the US.

Search for ENLIGHTMENT. Technological advances in early XXth century marked them, they wanted to make philosophy more 'scientific' by embracing 'objetivity'. Experimental testing -> compare data and theory without biases. Surest route to the truth
